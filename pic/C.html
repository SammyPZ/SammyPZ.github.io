WHAT DID I LEARN ABOUT MY COMMUNITY?

MY NEIGHBORHOOD

My neighborhood is called Villa Rosa. It's quite big. My neighborhood has always been a quiet and lively place. Its people have always been very kind, kind-hearted and generous. There are not many shops, restaurants, bookstores, or pastry shops nearby. Nearby there are courts, churches, ice cream parlors, hairdressers, shoe stores, etc.

PROBLEMS IN MY COMMUNITY
ABANDONED ANIMALS ILLEGALLY PARKED CARS POLLUTION

In my community, I saw that there are many animals abandoned on the streets, they have no housing, no food and no medicine.

In my community, I could see that there are many cars parked in forbidden places, and nobody has done anything to help.

In my community the great environmental contamination, air pollution, noise pollution, among others, is evident.
